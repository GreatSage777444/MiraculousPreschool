Admission Information

We welcome families who are looking for a nurturing preschool experience built on love, learning, and strong values.

🧒 Eligibility

Admissions are open for Playgroup, Nursery, LKG, and UKG based on the age and readiness of the child.

📋 Simple Process

Our admission process is easy and parent-friendly, with guidance available at every step.

🏫 School Visit

Parents are encouraged to visit our preschool, interact with our team, and experience our environment.

💖 Personal Support

We are happy to answer questions and help you choose the best program for your child.

Admission Process

1️⃣ Enquiry

Contact us by phone, WhatsApp, email, or by visiting the preschool to know more about our programs.

2️⃣ School Visit

Visit our preschool to explore the environment, meet our team, and understand our approach.

3️⃣ Application

Fill in the admission form with the required child and parent details.

4️⃣ Confirmation

Complete the formalities and confirm your child’s admission to begin their learning journey with us.

Documents Required

Please keep the following documents ready during admission.

📄 Child Documents

Birth certificate, passport-size photographs, and any previous school records if applicable.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Details

ID proof, address proof, and contact information of parents or guardians.

🩺 Medical Information

Basic health details, allergies, or any important medical information for the child’s safety.

📝 Additional Forms

Any school-specific forms or declarations required at the time of admission.

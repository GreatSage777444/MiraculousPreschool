Our Programs

Age-appropriate learning experiences designed to support every stage of your child’s early development.

Programs We Offer

Each program is carefully planned to build confidence, communication, creativity, social skills, and school readiness through joyful learning.

Playgroup

Age: 1.5 – 3 years

A gentle introduction to preschool in a warm, playful, and secure environment.

  1. Language development through rhymes, stories, and basic vocabulary
  2. Oral counting from 1 to 10 and recognition of shapes and colors
  3. Sensory play with sand, water, and textured materials
  4. Fine motor development through scribbling, clay play, and simple activities
  5. Social habits like greeting, sharing, and following simple instructions
  6. Life skills such as toilet training, eating habits, and self-care routines

Nursery

Age: 3 – 4 years

A joyful learning stage where children build early academic and social foundations.

  1. Recognition of letters A–Z and introduction to phonics
  2. Oral and visual recognition of numbers from 1 to 20
  3. Introduction to body parts, animals, fruits, and vegetables
  4. Shape and color identification with sorting and matching activities
  5. Rhymes, storytelling, and picture-based conversations
  6. Development of classroom discipline and essential life skills

LKG

Age: 4 – 5 years

A strong step forward in literacy, numeracy, general awareness, and confidence building.

  1. Writing A–Z and reading simple 2–3 letter words
  2. Number writing from 1 to 50 and introduction to addition concepts
  3. Basic EVS topics such as family, plants, seasons, and animals
  4. Recognition of opposites, days of the week, and weather concepts
  5. Show & Tell activities and memory-building games
  6. Practice of manners, hygiene, and active classroom participation

UKG

Age: 5 – 6 years

A comprehensive school-readiness program that strengthens academics and everyday life skills.

  1. Reading and writing simple sentences with basic grammar awareness
  2. Number writing from 1 to 100, basic addition and subtraction, and tables of 2 and 3
  3. Environmental awareness including festivals, transport, and community helpers
  4. Understanding healthy habits and personal safety concepts
  5. Vocabulary building through stories, flashcards, and games
  6. Confidence-building through role play, group activities, and simple assessments

Playgroup

(Age: 1.5 – 3 years)

  1. Language development through rhymes, stories, and basic vocabulary
  2. Oral counting 1 to 10 and recognition of shapes and colors
  3. Sensory play with sand, water, and textured materials
  4. Development of fine motor skills through scribbling and clay play
  5. Social habits like greeting, sharing, and following instructions
  6. Life skills including toilet training, eating habits, and self-care

Nursery

(Age 3 - 4)

  1. Recognition and phonic sounds of A–Z
  2. Oral and visual recognition of numbers 1 to 20
  3. Introduction to body parts, animals, fruits, and vegetables
  4. Shape and color identification with sorting activities
  5. Rhymes, storytelling, and picture-based conversations
  6. Development of classroom discipline and basic life skills

LKG

(Age 4 - 5)

  1. Writing A–Z and reading simple 2–3 letter words
  2. Number writing 1 to 50 with introduction to addition concepts
  3. Basic EVS topics like family, plants, seasons, and animals
  4. Recognition of opposites, days of the week, and weather
  5. Introduction to Show & Tell and memory games
  6. Practice of manners, hygiene, and classroom participation

UKG

(Age 5 - 6)

  1. Reading and writing simple sentences with correct grammar usage
  2. Number writing 1 to 100, basic addition and subtraction, tables of 2 & 3
  3. Environmental awareness including festivals, transport, and community helpers
  4. Understanding of good touch & bad touch and healthy habits
  5. Vocabulary building through stories, flashcards, and games
  6. Confidence-building through role play, group activities, and assessments

About Us

Miraculous Preschool* is more than just a school—it's a second home where your child begins a lifelong journey of learning with joy and confidence. Founded with a passion for nurturing young minds, we focus on creating a secure, playful, and stimulating environment where children feel loved, valued, and empowered. We believe that the early years lay the foundation for a child’s future. That’s why our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to promote creativity, communication, curiosity, and character. With a perfect blend of academics, life skills, and values, we aim to shape confident individuals who are ready to face the world with positivity. Our team of experienced educators and caregivers are dedicated to ensuring every child receives personal attention, encouragement, and support. We value the partnership of parents and work together to track every milestone of your child’s growth.

Our dedicated team of educators provides a safe and stimulating environment for children to explore, learn, and develop essential skills.

We believe in fostering a love for learning through play, hands-on activities, and individualized attention.

Join us in creating a strong foundation for your child's future!
